Here's what's important for you to know about financial aid:

If you have or will apply for financial aid through the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA®), your funding may include loans (which you must pay back) and grants (which you do not have to pay back**).

Please note: You will be responsible for paying any remaining balance after the disbursement of any loans or grants for which you qualify.

If you’re using financial aid, you will receive an offer. Please review this offer carefully. If you have any questions about your offer, please call Student Financial Services at 877.455.7648.

**Students who fail to complete the academic period for which the federal Pell Grant was awarded may be asked to pay back a portion of the grant.
